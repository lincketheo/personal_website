About Theo Lincke

I'm a Software Engineer with a focus on clean software and system architecture, driven by a love for breaking problems down into small tangible units. My background spans digital signal processing, and full-stack development, and I have a deep commitment to building secure, reliable software. I subscribe to the Unix philosophy (keeping things simple, and building software tools that work in tandem with one another).

Outside of work, I stay active-country swing dancing at Clarendon Ballroom in Arlington, hiking and camping off-grid, and spending time lifting or swimming. I also enjoy woodworking and diving into a good fantasy novel.
